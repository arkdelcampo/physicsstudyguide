WORK

FORMULA

w = Fd

UNITS

  • w = work (J)
  • F = magnitude of force (N)
  • d = distance (m)

INFO AND KEY TERMS

In order to do work, you need to apply a force to move an object. When you do this, you are transferring your energy into the object.

  • Work - Transfer of energy from one object to another OR conversion of energy from one form to another.
  • Energy - The ability to do work.
