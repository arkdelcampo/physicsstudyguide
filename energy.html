ENERGY

KINETIC ENERGY FORMULA

Ek = 1 ⁄ 2 mv2

UNITS

  • Ek = kinetic energy (J)
  • m = mass (kg)
  • v = speed (m/s)

POTENTIAL ENERGY FORMULA

Ep = mgh

UNITS

  • Ep = potential energy (J)
  • m = mass (kg)
  • g = gravity (m/s2)
  • h = height (m)

MECHANICAL ENERGY FORMULA

Ek = Ep

UNITS

  • Ek = kinetic energy (J)
  • Ep = potential energy (J)

INFO AND KEY TERMS

There are many types of energy which can be grouped into two categories:

  • Kinetic - Energy due to motion
  • Potential - Energy stored in an object (due to state/position)

Mechanical Energy is the sum of an object's energy (kinetic + potential). The total mechanical energy will be the same throughout the whole system.
