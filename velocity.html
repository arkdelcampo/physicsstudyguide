VELOCITY

FORMULA

v = d/t

UNITS

  • v = average speed (m/s)
  • d = distance travelled (m)
  • t = time elapsed (s)

INFO AND KEY TERMS

Velocity is a quantity that designates how fast and in what direction a point is moving.

  • Uniform Motion - Moving at a constant rate in the same direction.
  • Average Speed - A change in distance over a given time interval.
